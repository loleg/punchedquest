About this project

A turn based role playing game where you plan a sequence of actions - optionally using printed "punch" cards - to solve a quest.

Tick off all the actions you would like the character to take, top to bottom, and click Punch it! to go through the steps. You can also Print out a paper card and Scan it in with your phone to set up the sequence. This is just a prototype, but we are working on some devious, exciting levels ;)

Built with: HTML5, RPG JS, Bootstrap, jQuery, Ruby, Sinatra and Mork.
